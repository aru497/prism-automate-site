Prism Automate is the AI arm of the Prism family — the Bengaluru crew behind Prism Scale and DesignHQ. The answer was one home, three divisions, zero chaos — and 589+ projects later, for teams from PhonePe and Accenture to Air India and Brookfield, one lesson kept repeating: the teams that pull ahead are the ones that automate.
