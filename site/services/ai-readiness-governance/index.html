The opposite. The point is guardrails that let teams move faster with confidence — usage policies, review gates, and escalation paths that satisfy legal without becoming a bottleneck.

We're in a regulated industry — can you handle that?

Yes. We build the risk register and paper trail around your specific obligations, so 'we use AI carefully' becomes something that survives an audit.

How long does the assessment take, and how disruptive is it?

Typically two to three weeks. The load on your teams is a handful of scheduled interviews and read-only access to the systems we review — no freeze on work, no tooling to install. You get the scored verdict and remediation plan at the end, not a months-long audit that outlives its own findings.
